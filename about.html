ABOUT

MISSION

The Collegiate Cephalopod Association aims to provide opportunities for college students to explore their interest in the Splatoon franchise and bring Splatoon 3 into the collegiate esports spotlight. We host tournaments and provide a platform for college students who enjoy competitive Splatoon, and we take pride in the safe and friendly space cultivated by the CCA community.

HISTORY

The CCA is the flagship collegiate Splatoon organization, beginning as a collaboration between students of UC San Diego and Purdue University who sought to provide their peers with resources and opportunities to form student organizations and compete. The Splatoon community is widely regarded as one of the most inclusive gaming communities, and it saw significant growth in the year following the release of Splatoon 3. Our staff is a team of students and alumni who have engaged with the community as esports athletes, creators, and leaders and are passionate about the game, the growth of its collegiate community, and fostering a welcoming space for students.
